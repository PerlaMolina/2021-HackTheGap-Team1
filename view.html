Rebellion Relief Fund

Accepts donations through: Credit Card and PayPal



Holistic Heaux has recently became a resource for the Black and Brown community within the Twin Cities area since the murder of George Floyd.

As soon as riots began in our city, we immediately thought of our most vulnerable: mothers/caregivers and children. Where the riots and protests occurred, there are dense populations of Black and Brown families. We support the uprising in all ways, which means ensuring the safety of our Black and Brown youth and women. This is how we began our mutual aid fund.
